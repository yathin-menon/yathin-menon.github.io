Learning Plan

  1. What is your long term goal and or career pathway?

    2. My long term goal is to be proficient at coding and integrate it into my full-time job. I was reading through the blog post submissions and found the above as a recurring pattern. There are always tech improvements and resources that makes life easier for the user and the organisation as a whole. Maybe due to limitations, funding or other factors, alot of these concerns or issues within the workplace go unexplored and left to stagnate

    Currently working within the banking and finance industry; there is an exceedingly high demand for automation mainly due to volume vs time concerns. My role is directly related to lending applications and the sheer number is often overwhelming.

    It is disappointing to realise that the software and applications being used till date is actually 20+ years old and often out-dated.

    In a sudden moment of clarity, I understood that coding and programming will alleviate the daily struggles of being a banker under pressure. Additionally, it would save the company a lot of time and money. Moreso, I consider myself a student forever and Dev-Academy has now paved the way to my success.
